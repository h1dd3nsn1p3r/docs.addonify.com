# Floating cart coupon settings

Coupons are applicable only if coupons are enabled in the WooCommerce setting page. Addonify Floating Cart has the feature to apply and remove those coupons to product items in cart.

## Display applied coupons

Enable this option to display the applied coupons in the cart modal coupon section.

## Auto hide screen when coupon is applied

Enable this option to automatically hide the coupon screen when a coupon is applied successfully.
