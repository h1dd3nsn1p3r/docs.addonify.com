# Quick view product

Customize how WooCommerce product appears in the quick view modal box.

## Product options

##### Product Thumbnail

Product thumbnail choice for product detail inside modal box.

##### Enable light box

Gallery trigger button over the product thumbnail to enable the lightbox.
