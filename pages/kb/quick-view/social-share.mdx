# Quick view pro social share

Add social sharing features to your Quick View popup modal. This feature allows customers to share your product on social media platforms.


## Social Share Options

general options related to social sharing

### Enable social share ( pro )

By enabling this feature, you can achieve the social sharing features on the Quick View popup modal.

[![Social share](/products/quick-view/social-share.png)](/products/quick-view/social-share.png)



### Social share title ( pro )

The title displayed before the social share icons.

[![Social share title](/products/quick-view/social-share-title.png)](/products/quick-view/social-share-title.png)



### Email subject ( pro )

Subject for email for sharing. This feature is only for mail sharing.
