# Quick view pro social share

Add social sharing features to your Quick View popup modal. This feature allows customers to share your product on social media platforms.



##### Enable social share ( pro )

Enable or disable the social sharing features on the Quick View popup



##### Social share title ( pro )

The title displayed before the social share icons.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/social-share-title.png)](/products/quick-view/social-share-title.png)



##### Email subject ( pro )

Subject for email for sharing. This feature is only for mail sharing.


##### Social icon size

Size for social share icons.



##### Social icon color ( pro )

Color for social share icons.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/social-share-icon-color.png)](/products/quick-view/social-share-icon-color.png)



##### Social icon background color ( pro )

Background Color for social share icons.
