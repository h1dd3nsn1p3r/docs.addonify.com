# Quick view pro related product

Add quick product navigation to your Quick View popup modal. This feature allows customers to view the details of the next and previous products without closing the Quick View popup.


##### Enable related products ( pro )

Enables or disables related product feature on quick view popup modal.


##### Related setion title ( pro )

Section title for the related product.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-section-title.png)](/products/quick-view/related-section-title.png)



##### Related products per row ( pro )

Specify how many items to display in one row the related products section.


##### Related products gap ( pro )

Specify the gap between related products on related products section.



##### Related products title color ( pro )

Product title color on related product section.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-product-title-color.png)](/products/quick-view/related-product-title-color.png)
