# Quick view pro related product

Add related product feature to your Quick View popup modal. This feature allows customers to view the related products of the current products on the Quick View popup modal.


## Related Product Options

### Enable related products ( pro )

By enabling this feature, you can display related product on quick view popup modal.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-product.png)](/products/quick-view/related-product.png)


### Related setion title ( pro )

Customize the section title for the related product.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-section-title.png)](/products/quick-view/related-section-title.png)


## Related Product Styles Options

### Related products per row ( pro )

Specify how many items to display in one row on the related products section. Default is four items per row.


### Related products gap ( pro )

Specify the gap between related products on related products section. Default is 30 pixels. 

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-product-gap.png)](/products/quick-view/related-product-gap.png)



### Related products title color ( pro )

Product title color on related product section.

[![Product meta](/products/quick-view/related-product-title-color.png)](/products/quick-view/related-product-title-color.png)
