# Compare products settings page

Managing the settings & options of the Addonify Compare Products plugin is easy. Once you have installed the plugin, you will find a menu item in your WordPress Dashboard labelled “Addonify“. Click the label to access the setting page of the Addonify Compare Products plugin.

[![Access plugin settings page](/products/compare-products/compare-products.png)](/products/compare-products/compare-products.png)



## Settings

Settings include all the settings related to the compare button, floating dock, search modal and comparision table. You can configure the settings & click the `Save Options` button at the top right corner to save your changes.

## Design

Design include all the designs as well as color options. You can change the way how the compare products components will appear on the front end of a website.
